Application Reasons

The U.S. Army Board of the welfare department has made the new rules for expatriates in war zones, that request work leave to be applied by their loved ones/friends/relatives for the reasons below.

HEALTH LEAVE

The health leave is the leave for personnels under the U.S Army who are not in good health. Leave benefits mainly involves proper healthcare and rest based on their potential prognosis.

CONJUGAL VACATION

This leave is granted based on marital grounds, as well as other strong conjugal reasons.

ANNUAL LEAVE

This is for active duty soldiers who fall under the qualified criteria.

NB: You must be informed that emergency leaves are not free of charge unless there is a life-threatening emergency. You are thereby expected to go through the leave categories to make a proper choice.
A leave duration is made available for you to choose and after you choose and send a payment according to the choice made for your partner depending on your capability and the expected time that you will want him to spend with you. The reasons for payment for emergency leave Application are to assist the USA military authority in replacement expenses and supporting troops coming to take over duties from anyone going on emergency leave and must be considered worthy and a way of ensuring that our troops are protected and allowed to judiciously make use of their times for reasons they have applied and paid for.
